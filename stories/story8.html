How I Celebrate My Tiny Achievements in Learning

For me, the only time that I only celebrated my tiny achievements in learning Japanese is when I complete my Japanese Content Adventure journey.

I celebrated it by eating Siopao or drinking milk tea with the flavor of Cookies and Cream. I also sometimes went to fast food chains like Jollibee, KFC, McDo and many more to eat food.

But I only went there because I have a purpose, it is to celebrate my achievements not because of money, I am actually very frugal about it. I only choose menu that are cheap and make me feel full.

Also, it serves as a memory that I explore different kinds of establishments and that I love exploring different kinds of foods. But Siopao and Milk tea are one of my favorite food on my lists.
