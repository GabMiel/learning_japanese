How I First Got Interested in Japanese

When I was a kid, I am already watching cartoons and anime in T.V, of course it's dub in the Philippines.

Growing up and now that I am a teenager, I first got hooked in watching anime, I started watching anime website online and my first anime that I watch online is "Detective Conan" and stop at around episode 1000.

After watching many different animes, I saw my uncle reading Manhwa, at that time I thought it was boring, but after months, I got influence and my curiosity got hooked so I try it.

My uncle intall an app called Tachiyomi in my phone and configure it, then he add a Manhwa called "Dice: The Cube That Changes Everything" it was a nice story, but I quitted halfway, lol.

After reading many manhwas, I delve into many different worlds and expand my horizon. My fantasy starting to connect to reality little by little. Until something pops up in my mind.

What if I started learning Japanese... It lingers in my mind for a long time, until I have a classmate who I just saw using Duolingo and he is learning Japanese just for fun. He influence me to try that too. That was Marx!

Then little by little, I started writing Hiragana in my notebook and some of my classmates saw me writing Japanese letters, it's embarrassing and kind of proud that someone notice what I am doing.

So yeah! as time goes by, I still play Duolingo and still trying to keep the momentum going. I also keep learning Japanese day by day and I hope I can meet Japanese people someday.
