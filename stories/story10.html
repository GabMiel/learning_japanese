The Reason I Want to Share My Japanese Journey Online

I want to share my Japanese journey online because it helps me stay motivated. I feel more responsible for my progress because I know I am documenting something that matters to me.

I also want to share it because I want to see how far I can go. When I look back one day, I want to remember the small steps I took and the moments that made me continue learning.

I get shy posting publicly because I worry about mistakes, but I still want to share my journey because it feels meaningful. It becomes a reminder that I tried and that I kept going.

I also share it because I hope someone out there might feel inspired to start their own learning journey. Even if only one person relates to it, that is already enough for me.

In the end, I share my Japanese journey online because it is part of who I am right now. It shows my effort, my growth, and my curiosity, and I want to remember all of it someday.
