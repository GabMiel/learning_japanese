Thinking About the People Who Might See My Work One Day

Sometimes I think about the people who might see my work one day because it feels strange to imagine someone reading the things I write in the future. Maybe it will be a friend, a classmate, or a stranger.

I get shy about posting publicly because I worry about how people might judge me. But I still write because a part of me hopes someone will understand what I am trying to say someday.

They might see my mistakes because my writing is simple and honest. But that is fine because these stories show who I am right now and what I was thinking during my Japanese journey.

If someone reads this in the future, I hope they feel the sincerity because I wrote everything with honesty. I hope it reminds them of how I tried and how I kept going even when I was unsure.

And if no one reads it, that is fine because what matters is that I created something. I left something behind that shows I tried and that I cared about my journey at this moment in my life.
