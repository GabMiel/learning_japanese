My First Japanese Music That I Transliterated

This will be a short story. It started back then in 2015, I really love watching anime called "Sword Art Online" in TV from the Philippines. My family watch it in the morning when I was a kid.

Then in 2021, it gives me reminiscence when I watch it again through online using smartphone. I remember the song and it was the song that my uncle didn't want to skip because it was so good.

That song is called "Crossing Fields" by LiSA. I started transliterating or creating my custom lyrics because of this music because I want to memorize this song and I like the song a lot.

When I am transliterating this I am shocked because my surname really is called in the song. You can say that it was my signature song because it's the song with my name on it.

So yeah, this song is memorable to me because it's the first song I transliterated and it have my name on it. Thanks for reading I guess.
